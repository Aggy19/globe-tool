Recording notes

  • Hit Record above — it captures the globe + stars at the phone’s native resolution and saves an .mp4 (H.264) straight to your downloads.
  • For a seamless background loop, tick Auto-stop after one full loop, then drag the file into a Figma frame as a fill and set it to loop.
  • 8–12 Mbps looks great and stays small (space compresses well); the “100 MB (Figma)” cap keeps you inside Figma’s limit.
  • Star background off = flat dark field you can key out in Figma.
